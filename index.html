About Me

Hey! I'm Dylan Smith, a junior at Iowa State University studying software engineering. I am currently interning at Hy-Vee HST as a software engineer.

I've had an interest in technology for as long as I can remember. It wasn't until high school, during some introduction to programming classes, that I realized I wanted to follow the software engineer career path. I've learned a ton about the software engineering field during my time at Iowa State, and want to continue to learn as I follow my career path. Currently I work for Hy-Vee's technology center, HST, doing full stack web development.

Other than writing code, music is a huge passion of mine. You'll catch me at any local show I can find. I even play lead guitar in a band from Ames, Iowa. We don't have any music out yet, but keep an eye out for our debut new EP.

Some technologies I've worked with are: C/C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, C#/.NET, Python, Java. This website showcases some implementations of these technologies.
